About Us

Elias Engineering Works, established in 1976, is a leading manufacturer of a broad range of aluminum casting products. We offer:

  • Aluminum Brass Casting
  • Aluminium Die Casting
  • Aluminum Sand Casting Parts
  • Aluminum Pressure Die Casting Machine

Under the leadership of Mr. Herman Dsouza, we have achieved excellence in providing high-quality products that are corrosion-resistant, high-performance, and long-lasting.
